AN ELDERLY man from Eastleigh has been missing since yesterday morning.

Police are desperately appealing for information on the whereabouts of Gerald Haines.

The 76-year-old was last seen in the High Street at 10am on February 1.

He is described as being white, around 5ft 7ins tall, of slim build and he has a tattoo of an anchor on one arm. He has grey hair and a grey beard.

Mr Haines was last wearing a black jacket with fur collar, burgundy top, dark jeans and brown shoes. He was also carrying a black bag.

If you have seen Gerald or have any information on his whereabouts, then call 101, quoting the reference 44220044580.
