Johnson and Morawiecki will arrive in Kyiv tomorrow

From a week ago: Baltic-Black Sea Axis: military union of Britain, Poland and Ukraine

British Prime Minister Boris Johnson and Polish Prime Minister Mateusz Morawiecki plan to announce a new format of cooperation with Ukraine during their visit to Kyiv as MP Oleksiy Honcharenko reported…

“According to my information, the British Prime Minister Boris Johnson is coming to Ukraine as early as tomorrow. At the same time, Polish Prime Minister Mateusz Morawiecki will also be here on a working visit. There is a possibility that they will sign a declaration regarding a new union,” Honcharenko wrote.

We remind that UK Prime Minister Boris Johnson will pay an official visit to Ukraine tomorrow. Also on February 1, Polish Prime Minister Mateusz Morawiecki will arrive in Ukraine.

Moreover, French Foreign Minister Jean-Yves Le Drian has announced a visit to Kyiv with his counterpart from Germany, Annalena Baerbock, on February 7-8.
