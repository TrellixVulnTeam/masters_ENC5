Police are becoming increasingly concerned after a man has been missing for days from his Scots home.

Gordon Wilson, aged 51, was last seen in the Dumfries area at around 11am on Monday, January 31.

He was reported missing on Tuesday, February 1, and officers want to locate him as soon as possible.

The 51-year-old is known to travel throughout Dumfries and Galloway.

Gordon has also previously been traced to both Ayrshire and Renfrewshire since being reported missing.

He has been described as white, around 5ft 7ins in height and of slim build with receding shoulder-length grey and brown hair.

Police Scotland are asking anyone with information or Gordon himself to get in touch to ensure that he is safe and well.

Inspector Morgan said: “Gordon has been missing before however we are becoming concerned for his wellbeing. We want to trace him as soon as possible to ensure that he is safe and well.

"We know that he travels throughout the Dumfries and Galloway areas and has previously been traced in Ayrshire and Renfrewshire after being reported missing

“Finally, we would appeal to Gordon himself to get in touch. Whilst he may not want people to know where he is, we have to make sure he is safe and well.”

Anyone with information is asked to contact Police Scotland via 101, quoting reference no. 1167 of Tuesday, February, 1.
