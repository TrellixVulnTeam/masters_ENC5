he 2022 Winter Olympics are almost upon us as China picks up the baton from South Korea and PyeongChang, which hosted the Games four years ago.

It feels like only yesterday that we were watching the action of an epic summer unfold in Tokyo, those Games delayed by a year because of the coronavirus pandemic, but six months on all eyes are instead on Beijing.

The Winter Olympics are officially slated to run from February 4-20, 2022, but the action actually gets underway two days earlier, on February 2, with preliminary rounds in some sports.
