MANILA, Philippines (The Adobo Chronicles, Manila Bureau) – Ex-Comelec Commissioner Rowena Guanzon lashed out at her critics, defending her leaked vote to disqualify Ferdinand ”Bongbong” Marcos from running for President in the May 2022 Philippine elections.

”Kahit magdildil pa ako ng asin,” (even if I have lick salt), Guanzon said she will fight for honor and excellence, practically adding inSALT to injury.

Meanwhile, in a recent interview with broadcaster Karen Davila, the feisty Guanzon said she has a lot of money to be able to roam the country and campaign against (presumably) Bongbong Marcos. adding contradiction to her statements.
