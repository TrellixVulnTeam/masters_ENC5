CHINESE New Year is here and more than one billion people around the world will celebrate the event with parties, fireworks and more.

The Chinese New Year - also known as the Spring Festival or Lunar New Year - lasts for 16 days, starting from Chinese New Year's Eve to the Lantern Festival.

The Lunar calendar has 12 Chinese zodiac animal signs and 2022 will be a Year of the Tiger.

The Tiger is the third sign of the Chinese Zodiac, which also features the Ox, Rat, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Pig.
