Hamburg police have tweeted that, at this stage, it remains unclear whether the armed individual entered the school or simply passed by.

Hamburg police say: "A youth armed with a gun is said to have entered the school building. It is not clear whether the gunman observed actually entered the building or just walked "in the direction of the school building" and then perhaps past the school. As a precaution, the school building is currently being searched by special units."
