A Southampton woman died in hospital after a fall at her home, an inquest heard.

Pamela Audrey Wedge, of Wonston Road, died on December 20 at Southampton General Hospital.

Ms Wedge, 90, was taken to hospital following the fall when she hurt her ankle and suffered other injuries.

After this she deteriorated and died.

The causes of death were given as hospital acquired pneumonia, spinal injury, frailty of old age, and parkinsons.

Area coroner Jason Pegg adjourned the inquest at Winchester Corner's Court to be held on July 27.
