Video of the F35-C Crash With the Pilot Experiencing the Side Effects of the Vaxxine

A video and photo of the incident in the South China Sea with the failed landing of the F35-C has appeared.

The U.S. Navy has confirmed the authenticity of photos and videos circulating on social media showing the emergency landing of a $100 million F-35C stealth fighter on the USS Carl Vinson last Monday, causing the pilot to eject and the plane crash. in the sea. Seven sailors were injured, the pilot was lifted by a rescue helicopter and hospitalized.

As we wrote earlier, the accident is presumably due to the sharply deteriorating health of the vaccinated pilot, who is experiencing severe chest pain at the time of landing.
