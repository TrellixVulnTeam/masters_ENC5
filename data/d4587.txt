FORECASTERS have revealed the exact date snow will fall THIS WEEK - as ferocious Storm Corrie brings a bone-chilling freeze to Britain.

And the latest storm will bring the white stuff with it, as snow is set to fall in parts of Cumbria as early as Friday.

As the day goes on, areas just south of Manchester will see flurries too, WXCharts weather maps predict.

It comes after the Met Office issued fresh danger to life warning as Storm Corrie brought 92mph winds to the UK - just hours after Malik killed three people over the weekend..

Severe gusts have left 30,000 people without power in Scotland on top of the 7,000 who were already cut off as a result of Storm Malik.
