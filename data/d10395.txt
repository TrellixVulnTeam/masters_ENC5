Athletes and team officials are testing positive for Covid at much higher rates than other people arriving in China for the Beijing Olympics, organizers said Tuesday. Figures released showed 11 positive tests for Covid among 379 athletes and officials. They have been taken into isolation hotels and could miss their events.

The positive test rate of 2.9% for athletes and officials compared to 0.66% for Olympic “stakeholders,” a group which includes workers and media. There were 1,059 people in that category.
