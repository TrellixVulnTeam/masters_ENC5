The world's largest home improvement retailer said Tuesday that job applicants "could receive an offer within one day of applying."

said the said the "accelerated" hiring process is part of its plan to hire more than 100,000 new associates ahead of its busy spring season. The company is hiring flexible, full-time and part-time positions in customer service, sales, store support, freight, merchandising and warehouse associates.

The move by Home Depot underscores the difficulty businesses are facing as they try to fill open positions.
