Christmas arrived early for Wiltshire-based mum of one Adele Keeble, after she beat off hundreds of crisp lovers to be crowned the nation's 'Quavers superfan' by Walkers today, winning a lifetime supply of the floaty, light snack and becoming the first person in the world to try the retro Prawn Cocktail and Salt & Vinegar Quavers, before their hotly anticipated January return after a decade off shelves.
