Authorities in Florida charged 68 drug dealers who sold narcotics on popular gay dating apps after detectives found it ‘relatively easy’ to get them to incriminate themselves by messaging them undercover on Grindr. The six-month-long investigation, dubbed ‘Swipe Left for Meth,’ was launched by the Polk County Sheriff’s Department after they received an anonymous tip. ‘It was a shock to us that they were openly advertising.’
