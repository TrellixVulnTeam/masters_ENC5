The Super Bowl is the biggest game in American sport, one of the most-watched matches in the TV sporting calendar.

This year's event is the 56th time the game has been staged, so, in accordance with Roman numerical tradition, it will be known as Super Bowl LVI.

The game pits the winners of the AFC and NFC conferences against one another in a game of four quarters of 15 minutes which somehow conspires to take about four hours, including the much anticipated half-time show.
