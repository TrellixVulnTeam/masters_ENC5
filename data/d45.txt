Next year: State and local government leaders will make their case to a new administration after failing to win federal government relief this time around. President Trump and other Republicans balked at the aid, saying Democratic-led states and cities were looking for a bailout after years of fiscal mismanagement. Mayor Lightfoot says the pandemic erased $800 million in revenue this year, and the bulk of the $1.2 billion deficit she had to close in next year’s budget is tied to projected shortfalls as the pandemic wears on. For his part, Gov. Pritzker recently announced a $700 million round of spending cuts to begin dealing with a $3.9 billion deficit — $2 billion of it tied to lost tax receipts during the pandemic.
