POLICE arrested a 15-year-old girl earlier today after an incident in which care workers were allegedly assaulted.

The suspect was further arrested on suspicion of resisting arrest and four counts of assaulting an emergency worker.

Police say no-one was seriously injured.

Posting on social media Fareham Cops said: "This morning police were called to an incident by care workers reporting they had been assaulted by a resident.

"A 15-year-old female was arrested on suspicion of six counts of assault.

"She was further arrested on suspicion of one count of resisting arrest and four counts of assaulting an emergency worker, three of these occurring whilst in police custody.

"Fortunately nobody received serious injuries and all officers were able to stay on duty to complete the rest of their shift. The investigation continues."
