We could be heading into a “sell everything” moment when markets across the board correct sharply. Technical analyst Chris Vermeulen shares how investors may want to prepare for such an occurrence.

He is bullish on gold and silver for the next few years. Precious metals are in a “super-cycle,” he says.

Chris Vermeulen has been involved in the markets since 1997 and is the founder of Technical Traders Ltd. He is an internationally recognized technical analyst, trader, and is the author of the book: 7 Steps to Win With Logic.
