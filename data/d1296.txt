A woman from Southampton died after falling down a flight of stairs, an inquest heard.

Diana Elizabeth Chappell, of Barry Road, died in Southampton General Hospital on January 4.

Ms Chappell, 88, fell suffered a fracture to her spine as a result of the fall.

A post mortem was carried out by Dr Sanjay Jogai and found the causes of death to be: polytrauma, fall from stairs and bilateral bronchopneumonia.

Area coroner Jason Pegg adjourned the full inquest at Winchester Coroner's Court to take place on August 15.
