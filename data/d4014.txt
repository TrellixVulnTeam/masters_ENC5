FIREFIGHTERS rushed to a vehicle "well alight" on a public footpath this morning.

A team based at Totton were called to Bury Road in Marchwood shortly after 6am.

The vehicle was on a public footpath, and was completely destroyed in the blaze.

A spokesperson for Hampshire Fire and Rescue Service said: "Totton firefighters were called to a vehicle well alight on Bury Road in Marchwood shortly after 6am this morning.

"It was extinguished by a firefighter wearing breathing apparatus using one hose reel jet.

"Police were informed of the incident prior to HIWFRS leaving the scene at 06.42am."

Hampshire Constabulary has confirmed the incident is not being treated as suspicious.
