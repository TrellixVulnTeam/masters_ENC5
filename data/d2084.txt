Britain plans to double its military presence in NATO countries

The UK is considering a major deployment of its military forces to NATO countries as part of a plan to strengthen European borders….This is reported by Voice of America ​​with reference to the country’s government.

“I have ordered our armed forces to prepare to deploy across Europe next week to make sure we can support our NATO allies,” British Prime Minister Boris Johnson said.

…Johnson will consider options…including a new deployment of NATO troops.

In addition, the UK plans to double the number of its NATO troops stationed in Estonia. Boris Johnson ordered the military to “prepare for deployment across Europe”…and reassure NATO members on the eastern flank.

Recently, the media wrote that the UK is considering sending troops to Slovakia to strengthen NATO forces in Eastern Europe.
