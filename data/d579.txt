In perhaps the biggest news, Dele Alli swapped Tottenham for Everton and followed both Frank Lampard and Donny van de Beek through the door at Goodison Park. Elsewhere, league leaders Manchester City wrapped up a deal for highly-rated forward Julian Alvarez, who will remain at River Plate on loan.

Newcastle United finished up their rebuild by landing Matt Targett and Dan Burn while Spurs brought in Juventus duo Dejan Kulusevski and Rodrigo Bentantcur. Christian Eriksen, meanwhile, signed for Brentford.
